Fabian Ziems

Singer/Actor

My passion for acting and singing began at an early age. I first stepped onto a big stage when I was just nine years old and discovered my love for it. Since then, I`ve gained experience both on stage and in front of the camera, and I`ve already appeared on television. In addition to performing at major theaters such as the Festspielhaus Neuschwanstein in Füssen, I`m part of the Joy of Voice ensemble, with which I regularly perform at various events. I also sing cover songs, which you can listen to on my social media channels or experience me performing live there. I made my first television appearance as a singer in 2025, when I competed on the 13th season of the German singing competition show The Voice Kids.

Career History

Photo: Zum Glück zurück - all in production / ZDF

In 2020, Fabian played his first role as Lenny Sandmeier in the ZDF film "Zum Glück zurück". This was followed by other films and series on ZDF and ARD.

In the musical “Ludwig” performed at the Festspielhaus Neuschwanstein, Fabian played the role of Crown Prince Ludwig from 2019 to 2020.
